We welcome your participation in the APEC Workshop on Examining Measures that Affect Digital Trade in Services, bringing together government officials, private sector representatives, international organizations, and subject matter experts to examine policy and regulatory measures that shape digital services trade across the Asia-Pacific.

APEC member economies are invited to nominate self-funded participants for this workshop by July 24, 2026 (Singapore Standard Time). Economy participants must also complete official SOM3 registration through their economy's APEC Delegation Accreditation Officer by July 31, 2026.

If attendees are not attending through an APEC economy delegation, please work with the Project Overseers on SOM3 registration. Failure to complete APEC SOM3 registration will result in prohibition from the event.

For additional information regarding participant nominations, accreditation, workshop logistics, agenda, or technical content, please contact the appropriate point of contact below.
