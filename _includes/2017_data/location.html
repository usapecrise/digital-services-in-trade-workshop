The APEC Workshop on Examining Measures that Affect Digital Trade in Services will be held in person on August 22, 2026 at the Dalian International Conference Center in Dalian, China, on the margins of the Third APEC Senior Officials' Meeting (SOM3).

The venue is located in Dalian's Donggang Business District, approximately 30 minutes from Dalian Zhoushuizi International Airport (DLC). Additional venue information, workshop logistics, and access instructions will be shared with confirmed participants in advance of the workshop.
