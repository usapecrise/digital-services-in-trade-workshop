About

The APEC Workshop on Examining Measures that Affect Digital Trade in Services will bring together policymakers, government officials, private sector representatives, international organizations, and subject matter experts to examine policy and regulatory measures affecting digital services trade across the Asia-Pacific. Through expert presentations, economy experiences, and interactive discussion, participants will explore approaches that support innovation, economic growth, secure connectivity, efficient digital services trade, and other economy-level policy objectives
